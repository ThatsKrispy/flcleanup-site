If Disaster Strikes

What to do right now

1
Stay Safe

Keep clear of standing water near outlets or appliances. If the structure feels unsafe, get everyone out first.

2
Stop the Source

If it's safe, shut off the water main or the source of the leak to limit how far the damage spreads.

3
Document It

Take photos and video before any cleanup. This protects your insurance claim and speeds up approval.

4
Call FL Cleanup

Reach us 24/7 at 877-224-2532. We'll guide you and dispatch the nearest crew.
